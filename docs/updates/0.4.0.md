Connecting brushes can now throw their threads much further. The Reach dial goes all the way to 1000 px - five times its old limit - so big, sweeping webs that span the whole canvas are suddenly easy to draw.

- Raise **Reach** up to 1000 px for canvas-spanning webs
- Made for large canvases and loose, airy compositions
