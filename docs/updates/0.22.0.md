Bloom pads a stroke with extra scattered points, so even a quick mark fills out into a full web. Now you can set how far that scatter reaches - keep it tight around your line for a soft halo, or open it up to flood a wider area with texture.

- New Bloom radius dial: choose how far Bloom scatters its dots from your stroke
- Tight for a soft halo along the line, wide to fill a broader area
- Appears under Bloom once Bloom is turned on
