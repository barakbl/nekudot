An early, experimental way to export your whole drawing session as a video - a timelapse of the artwork building up, as MP4 or WebM. Switch on session recording in Application settings before you start drawing; when you're done, the Record row becomes Create process Video and replays everything you drew into a video file. It is still a work in progress and not finished - the results, and the option itself, may change or be removed in a later version - so treat it as a preview, not a settled feature. (With recording off, Record still makes a short GIF, just like before.) As always, it happens in your browser; nothing is uploaded.

- Experimental: an early preview that may change or be removed in a later version
- Export a whole-session timelapse as a video (MP4 or WebM) when recording was on
- Turn on recording in Application settings before you draw, then export when you finish
- Runs entirely in your browser - nothing is uploaded
