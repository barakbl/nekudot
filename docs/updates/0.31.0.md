Meet Glow, a new web style whose strands add light instead of stacking like paint. Where your web overlaps it brightens, and where different colours cross they mix toward a glowing white - so a dense stroke or a mandala turns into a soft, luminous halo on the dark canvas. Pick Glow under the Web styles and try it with a rainbow or gradient colour for the full effect. And there is a new "Add" mode on the Blend dial for when you want the glow even hotter.

- Glow: a new web style whose strands add light instead of stacking
- Overlaps brighten, and crossing colours mix toward white - a luminous halo
- Loveliest with a rainbow or gradient colour on the dark canvas
- A new "Add" blend mode pushes the glow even punchier
