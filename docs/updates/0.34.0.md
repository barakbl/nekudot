Now you can see exactly how big your brush is before you lay down a stroke. A ring follows your cursor at the brush's real painted size, scaling as you zoom so it always matches what will land. For connecting brushes there's a second, dashed ring that shows how far the web will reach out. Prefer the classic crosshair? Choose your cursor in App settings.

- A ring under your cursor shows the brush's real size, matched to your zoom
- A dashed ring shows how far a connecting web will reach
- Pick your style in App settings: size ring, reach ring, crosshair, or a mix
