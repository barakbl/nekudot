Your Layers panel now opens right from the toolbar, next to Memory Maps. Tap the stacked-layers icon and it drops open just below - no window to hunt for or drag around, which is a real relief on a tablet. Everything's still there: reorder by dragging the grip, rename a layer, set its opacity, duplicate or delete it, and choose the background. Tap the icon again, tap away, or press Escape to put it away. It's the next step in moving Nekudot's floating panels into the toolbar, so your tools stay a tap away wherever you draw.

- Open Layers from a stacked-layers icon in the toolbar, next to Memory Maps
- Drag to reorder, rename, opacity, duplicate, delete, background - all unchanged
- Tap to open, tap away to dismiss: friendlier on tablets and touch
