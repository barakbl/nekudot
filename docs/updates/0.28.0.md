Keeping what you made is now right there on the toolbar - no more digging through a menu. Tap Share to hand your drawing straight to your phone's share sheet (Instagram, Messages, save to Photos), or on desktop it saves a PNG and copies a caption ready to post. The more detailed options - a transparent PNG, the .nekudot file, a GIF - are still tucked in the "..." menu.

- A Share button now sits right on the toolbar, always one tap away
- On mobile it opens your share sheet; on desktop it saves a PNG and copies a caption
- All the detailed export options are still in the "..." menu
