On a narrow phone, the toolbar's dropdowns - the brush picker and the symmetry menu - could open half off the right edge, clipping the names. Now they gently slide back so the whole menu sits on screen, whatever size your display is. Nothing to change; it just fits.

- The brush and symmetry dropdowns no longer run off the edge on small screens
- Menus nudge themselves fully into view at any screen size
