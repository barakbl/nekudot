A few touches to make picking colours and learning brushes feel better. The colour picker updates your colour as you choose - no separate Apply step - and gradient connections now wrap seamlessly, with no hard seam where the colours loop. The brush Preview is one open playground, so you can just draw and explore.

- The colour picker applies live as you adjust; "Apply" is now a simple "Done"
- Gradient connection colours loop seamlessly, with no visible seam at the wrap
- Brush Preview is a single playground - draw freely to get a feel for any brush
- The Maps dot count refreshes correctly after you open a saved piece
