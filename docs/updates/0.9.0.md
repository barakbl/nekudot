Not sure what a slider does? Now you can watch it. Open the new Preview window from your brush settings, nudge any dial, and a demo stroke replays with your exact brush - so you see how the web weaves before you ever touch your own canvas.

- Open Preview from the settings panel (the eye next to Reset); every change replays a live demo at your real size, opacity, colour and style
- Flip between scenes - Wave, Circles, Spiral and Scribble - to see the effect on different shapes
- Switch to the Playground tab to draw freely and get a feel for the brush, then Clear and try again
- Set a Canvas, Light or Dark background to check contrast, and dial the playback speed to taste
- A "?" explains the setting you just changed, with a few tips to nudge you along
