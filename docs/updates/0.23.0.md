The toolbar's two combos - one for the brush, one for the connecting web - are now a single control. Pick Web and every connecting look lives right under it: Sketchy, Web, Bloomer, Fur, Lace and your own saved presets, as a tidy nested list. Choosing a style picks the brush and applies the look in one tap, and the style picker now sits inside the Web settings alongside its dials.

- The connecting brush is simply "Web", with every style nested under it
- Pick a style and it selects the brush and applies the look at once
- The style picker moved into the Web settings, so styles and their dials sit together
- One clear control in the toolbar instead of two
