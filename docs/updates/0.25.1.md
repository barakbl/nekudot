Your very first drawing opens on the mandala canvas, and now it stays readable while you play. A few eager strokes used to flood the centre into a solid white disc; the web is calmer now, so your kaleidoscope keeps its shape. Your own line shows faintly as you draw, too, so you can always see the mark you're making before the web blooms around it.

- The opening mandala no longer washes out when you scribble
- Your drawn stroke stays visible under the web
- A calmer, easier-to-steer web wherever you use the Bloomer style
