The Color Pen now varies its width by how fast you draw - a quick flick draws thin, a slow drag swells thick - so a mouse or finger gets the graceful, hand-driven weight a stylus gets from pressure. Its colour already walks the palette as you turn; now its weight follows your speed too, so direction and pace shape one expressive stroke. It's on by default; toggle it and tune the strength in Brush settings. A real stylus is unchanged - pressure still leads there.

- The Color Pen line thins as you move fast and swells as you slow down
- Grace for a mouse or finger - no pressure pen needed
- Direction moves the colour, speed moves the weight: one living stroke
- Toggle and tune it in Brush settings (Speed taper + Taper amount)
