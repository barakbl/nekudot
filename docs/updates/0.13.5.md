A big pass on touch and accessibility. Panels and small controls are now comfortable on tablets and touchscreens, and the whole toolbar - menus, style combos and colour swatches - works by keyboard and with screen readers. Clearer focus, contrast and labels make the app easier to read and steer for everyone.

- Panels and small controls are sized and spaced for touch and tablet
- Toolbar menus, combos and swatches are fully keyboard- and screen-reader friendly
- Dialogs trap focus and close with Escape; floating panels stay on-screen
- Sharper focus outlines, stronger contrast and clearer control names throughout
