Connect a folder once and Nekudot saves your artwork and settings right into it - no download dialogs, no clutter. Your work lives as real files on your own machine, ready to back up or open however you like. (Available in Chrome, which supports folder access.)

- Connect a local folder and save your artwork and settings there in one click
- Files land in your folder directly, with no save dialog each time
- If the browser ever forgets the folder, reconnect it in a single click
- Everything stays on your device - nothing is uploaded anywhere
