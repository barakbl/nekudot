You can now add Nekudot to your home screen and run it full screen, so it feels less like a website and more like an app you own - a bigger, calmer canvas with none of the browser's bars in the way. On an iPad or iPhone, tap Share, then Add to Home Screen: Nekudot gets its own icon and opens on its own, straight onto the canvas. And on an iPad or a desktop browser, a new Full screen button in the toolbar - next to Reset view - hides the browser's chrome so your art fills the whole display; tap it again, or press Esc, to come back. It's the same local app either way, and nothing is uploaded.

- Add Nekudot to your home screen for an app-like, chrome-free canvas - lovely on iPad
- A new Full screen button fills the display, wherever the browser supports it
- Its own icon, name and standalone window once installed
- Still entirely on your device - your work never leaves it
