Draw a curve with the Fur web and the hairs now sweep along the line you drew, instead of all combing one fixed way. Loop it, curl it, wind it around - the grain follows your contour like a real pelt, so fur finally reads as fur and not combed wallpaper. A new "Follow stroke" dial in the Fur settings sets how strongly it leans into your direction.
