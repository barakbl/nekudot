Squares and Circles are now a single Shapes brush. Pick it once, then switch between squares and circles with a two-button toggle right at the top of its settings - the same speed-sized stamps, fill and dash options as before, with one less item crowding the toolbar.

- Squares and Circles combined into one Shapes brush
- A quick Circles / Squares toggle at the top of its settings
- The same speed-sized stamps, fill colour and dash styles you already had
