It's easier to see - and to remember - how to move around your canvas. Zoom in or out and a small percentage pill flashes in the corner, so you always know where you are; click it to snap straight back to 100%. And the Shortcuts panel now has a View group that gathers every way to pan, zoom, rotate and reset the camera in one place.

- A zoom % pill flashes in the corner as you zoom - click it to jump back to 100%
- A new **View** group in Shortcuts collects every way to move the camera
- Pan, zoom, rotate and reset, all in one place to discover
