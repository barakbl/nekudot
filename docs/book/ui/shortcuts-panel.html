A live cheat-sheet of every key and gesture - and a few you can tap.

What it is

The in-app contents of Shortcuts & gestures, grouped by Brushes, Panels, Edit, Capture and Help. Open it with / or from the Windows menu. Each row is clickable - tapping it runs that action and leaves the panel open, which is how you reach keyboard-only actions on a touch device.

Notable rows

Toggle rows
Help hints shows a live on/off switch: flip it (or press ?) to reveal the small ? chips around the UI. On a phone this is how you turn help on - there's no ? key.
Record GIF
Under Capture - press r to record a few seconds of drawing.
Gestures
Two- and three-finger taps undo/redo; a four-finger swipe-up hides every panel.
