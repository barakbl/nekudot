Shortcuts & gestures

Every key and gesture in Nekudot.

Forget a key? Press / in the app at any time to pop up the built-in shortcuts cheat-sheet - the fastest way to find or remember a shortcut without leaving the canvas.

Help & reference

/Open / close the shortcuts cheat-sheet
?Toggle help hints (the “?” bubbles around the UI)

Panels

bToggle brush settings
lToggle layers
mToggle memory maps
yToggle symmetry (Tile / Radial / Mirror)
sToggle the “…” more menu
hHide / show all panels

The same panels are listed in the toolbar’s Windows menu.

Brushes

1Round
2Squares

Edit

⌘/Ctrl ZUndo
⌘/Ctrl ⇧ ZRedo

Gestures

Press & dragDraw a stroke (Round, Marker, Squares, Circles…)
Drag fasterSquares & Circles grow with stroke speed
Two-finger tapUndo (trackpad)
Three-finger tapRedo (trackpad)
Four-finger swipe upHide / show all panels (same as h)
Pen pressure & tiltShape the stroke with a stylus - see Pen & pressure
Drag a panel titleMove any panel (Brushes, Layers, Maps) around the screen
Try this You don't need to memorise these - just learn three: b for brush settings, m to flash the memory, and h to hide every panel for a clean, full-canvas view. The rest you can always summon with /.
