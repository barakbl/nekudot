Shapes key 2 / 3

Stamps speed-sized squares or circles along your stroke - pick which at the top of its settings.

What it does

As you drag, Shapes places a stamp spaced along the path - a square or a circle, whichever you have picked. The faster you move, the bigger each stamp (up to a cap), so a quick flick leaves big bold marks and a slow drag leaves a tight trail of small ones.

How to use

  • Press 2 or 3, or pick Shapes from the brush menu (under Other).
  • At the top of its settings, tap Circles or Squares to choose the shape.
  • Vary your speed to vary the size - that is the main expressive control.
  • Set Fill to none for outlines, or main/secondary for solid shapes.
Try this Flick fast for big bold stamps, then trail slowly for a tight run of small ones - one gesture can swell and taper like a brushstroke. Outline circles make bubble trails and dotted contours; filled squares make crisp mosaics.

Settings

Shape
Two buttons at the top of the settings: Circles or Squares.
Sensitivity
How strongly your stroke speed changes the stamp size.
Shape size
The smallest-to-largest size range; your speed picks a size between the two.
Fill
None (outline only), Main, or Secondary colour.
Fill opacity
Opacity of the filled shape.
Dash
Outline style: solid, dashed or dotted.
