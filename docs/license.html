Your work is yours

The license covers the software, not what you draw with it. Whatever you make in Nekudot is entirely yours - it never leaves your browser, and no one has any claim on it.

Standing on shoulders

Nekudot exists because other people shared their work first. With gratitude:

  • Harmony, by mrdoob - the connecting-line sketch tool that lit the spark.
  • d3-quadtree - finds each point's neighbours fast enough to weave in real time.
  • fflate & zod - pack and validate the .nekudot archive you save your work into.
  • Vite & TypeScript - the workbench it's built and bundled on.
  • Fraunces & Hanken Grotesk - the open-licensed typefaces this site is set in.

Special thanks

And to the people who shaped Nekudot along the way:

  • Silky - a friend and an amazing artist, for the inspiration, the ideas, and the help, and for testing the app on an ancient version of Safari 😄 Go see and follow her art
  • Alonbase - a friend and a wonderful mentor, whose ideas, guidance, and tireless effort helped make this tool the best it can be.
