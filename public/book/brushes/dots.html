Dots key 4

A grid-tap brush: one click stamps dots across a grid.

What it does

Dots is a grid brush. Each click is a single tap that stamps a dot at every grid intersection within reach of the cursor, with opacity fading toward the edge of the reach circle (how sharply is set by Opacity falloff — turn it to 0 for a flat, even stamp). Tap repeatedly to build density. Grid brushes don't feed the connecting map.

How to use

  • Press 4 or pick it from the brush menu.
  • Click (don't drag) to drop a burst of dots; click again to layer more.
  • Tune the grid with X / Y spacing and the falloff with Reach.

Settings

Dot radius
Size of each stamped dot.
X spacing / Y spacing
Distance between grid intersections horizontally / vertically.
Reach
Radius around the click that gets stamped; opacity falls off to the edge.
Tap strength
Maximum opacity at the center of a tap.
Opacity falloff
How sharply opacity fades toward the edge of the reach. 0 = flat (every dot at full strength); higher = steeper fade from center to edge.
