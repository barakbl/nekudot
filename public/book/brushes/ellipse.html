Ellipse key 6

A grid-tap brush that stamps ellipses — round to flat, at any angle.

What it does

Ellipse stamps an ellipse at each grid intersection within reach (with the same opacity falloff as Dots). The Ellipse slider morphs the shape from a perfect circle to a thin sliver, and Angle rotates it — handy for scales, petals, leaves and feather textures.

How to use

  • Press 8 or pick it from the brush menu.
  • Click to stamp a field of ellipses; rotate the Angle between taps for variation.
  • Set Fill to none for outline ellipses, or main/secondary for solid ones.

Settings

Size
The ellipse's major radius.
Ellipse
0 = round circle, up to 1 = flat sliver.
Angle
Rotation of each ellipse (0–180°).
Fill
None (outline), Main, or Secondary color.
X spacing / Y spacing / Reach / Tap strength / Opacity falloff
The shared grid-tap controls (see Dots). Opacity falloff at 0 stamps every ellipse at full strength; higher fades them toward the edge of the reach.
