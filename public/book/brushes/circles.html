◯Circles key 3

Like Squares, but round - speed-sized circles along the stroke.

What it does

Circles drops circles spaced along your path, each sized by your drawing speed (faster = larger, up to a cap). Great for bubble trails, dotted contours, or - with connecting on - circular nodes joined by a web.

How to use

  • Press 3 or pick it from the brush menu.
  • Change speed to change size; set Fill for solid vs. outline circles.
Try this Draw outline circles with a Connecting style on and you get bubble clusters joined by threads - lovely for molecules, constellations or foam. Vary your speed for a trail of mixed sizes.

Settings

Fill
None (outline only), Main, or Secondary color.
Fill opacity
Opacity of the filled circle.
Dash
Outline style: solid, dashed or dotted.
Connecting style
Optional web between circle centers - see connecting.
