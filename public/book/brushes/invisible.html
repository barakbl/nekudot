Invisible key 9

Leaves no ink — it only seeds the memory map.

What it does

The Invisible brush paints nothing permanent. As you drag, it drops points into the memory map and shows a brief glow where you went, then fades. The points stay in the cloud — so when you switch to a connecting brush, its web reaches toward the paths you traced invisibly.

How to use

  • Press 0 or pick it from the brush menu.
  • Trace the structure you want the web to follow (a face, a spiral, a horizon).
  • Switch to Classic Round or Web and draw — connections will gravitate to the seeded points.
Think of it as a guide layer made of points instead of pixels: invisible scaffolding for the connecting brushes.
