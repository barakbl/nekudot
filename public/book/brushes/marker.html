Marker key 8

A chisel-nib marker for solid, flat strokes.

What it does

Marker draws with an angled chisel tip, giving calligraphic strokes that are thick across and thin along the diagonal. By default it doesn't connect — it applies the No connect routing preset, so it behaves like a plain marker.

How to use

  • Press 9 or pick it from the brush menu.
  • Its opacity comes from the Opacity slider at the top of the brush settings panel (selecting Marker sets it to ~75%). Lower it to build up translucent layers.
  • Change the brush Size (also at the top of the settings panel) to widen the nib.

Settings

Opacity
Uses the global Opacity slider at the top of the brush settings panel (shared by every brush).
Connection & Connection art style
Off by default. Switch the Connection preset from No connect to Classic if you want the marker to weave a web too — see connecting.
