●Round key 1

A round-capped line that blooms into a soft, sketchy web.

What it does

Classic Round draws a continuous round line under your cursor, and - as it goes - connects each point to nearby points in the memory map. With its default Classic art style (faint, end-inset, brush-width lines), the connections pile into the hairy, pencil-sketch look that Harmony's sketchy brush is known for.

How to use

  • Pick it from the brush menu or press 1.
  • Selecting it applies the Classic connection art style and sets a low stroke opacity automatically.
  • Draw slowly for dense shading; flick for sparse threads.
  • Scribble back over an area to layer more connections and darken it.

Settings

Dash
Solid, dashed or dotted line for the stroke itself.
Connection & Connection art style
The two groups that control the web - see Memory maps & connecting.
Want a cleaner spider web instead of soft shading? Switch the Connection art style preset to Web, or just use the Web brush.
